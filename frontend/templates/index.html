About Pakistani Police

The Pakistani Police Force is dedicated to maintaining law and order, preventing and detecting crime, and ensuring the safety and security of all citizens across the nation.

With a rich history of service, our force comprises trained professionals committed to upholding justice and protecting the rights of every individual in Pakistan.

Mission

To serve and protect with integrity

Vision

A safer Pakistan for all
